Data Science & AI

Become a data-driven AI expert through our intensive bootcamp

Course Overview

Our Data Science & AI programme provides a comprehensive foundation in data analysis, machine learning and deep learning. Learn Python, statistics, data visualisation, and work with popular libraries such as pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, TensorFlow and Keras. Throughout the course, you’ll build real-world projects and explore ethical considerations in AI.

Hands-On Practice

Apply concepts through coding exercises, labs and capstone projects using real datasets.

Expert Mentorship

Learn from seasoned data scientists and AI engineers who guide you through complex topics.

Flexible Schedule

Choose part-time or full-time tracks and learn at your own pace with live sessions and recordings.

Curriculum

Data Science Foundations

Statistics, probability theory, Python programming and Jupyter notebooks.

Data Wrangling & Visualisation

Use pandas and NumPy for cleaning, and matplotlib/seaborn for exploratory analysis.

Machine Learning

Supervised & unsupervised learning, model evaluation and hyperparameter tuning with scikit-learn.

Deep Learning

Neural networks, CNNs and RNNs implemented using TensorFlow and Keras.

Capstone Project

Apply your skills to a real-world problem, from data collection to model deployment.
